2. How Users Can Request Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and data in either of the following ways:

2.1 In-App Deletion

  1. Open TaskSnap and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account & Data.
  3. Confirm deletion.

2.2 Email Request

Send an email to 6731503078@lamduan.mfu.ac.th
Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - TaskSnap
Include your account email address or login identifier.

3. Information Required for Email Requests

  • Account email address used in TaskSnap.
  • Sign-in method (Email, Google, Facebook, or Guest if known).
  • A clear statement requesting deletion of your account and associated data.

4. What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile data (email, display name, authentication provider).
  • Task data (title, notes, categories, dates, reminders, status).
  • User-uploaded or attached task content associated with your account (where applicable).

5. What Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited data temporarily if required for legal compliance, security or fraud prevention, and backup/recovery processes. Any retained data will be minimized and deleted after the required retention period.

6. Deletion Timeline

  • Deletion begins after identity/account verification.
  • Most requests are completed within 3 days.
  • In exceptional cases, deletion may take up to 30 days.

7. Verification and Security

To prevent unauthorized requests, we may verify that you own the account. This may include confirmation via your registered email address.
